About Me

PhD Candidate in NLP

I'm a PhD candidate in Natural Language Processing at the University of Bologna's LT-Lab, supervised by Prof. Paolo Torroni. My PhD journey began in November 2023 and is expected to conclude in November 2026.

Academic Background

  • MSc in Artificial Intelligence, University of Bologna (2021-2023)
  • BSc in Computer Science, University of Bologna (2018-2021)

Current Research Focus

My research explores AI-driven educational technology, focusing on automated generation of pedagogical materials via efficient language models. I specialize in leveraging QLoRA to fine-tune compact transformer models and RAG pipelines to integrate domain-specific knowledge. I also work in the field of argumentation and focus on the evaluation of open-ended generation, particularly assessing the reliability of LLMs as Judges.
