About Me

PhD Candidate in NLP

I'm a second-year PhD student in Natural Language Processing at the University of Bologna, Italy, where I focus on cutting-edge research in language understanding and generation. My PhD journey began in November 2023 and is expected to conclude in November 2026.

Academic Background

  • MSc in Artificial Intelligence, University of Bologna (2021-2023)
  • BSc in Computer Science, University of Bologna (2018-2021)

Current Research Focus

My research explores novel approaches in NLP, working closely with Zanichelli Editore to develop practical applications in educational technology. I'm particularly interested in educational exercise generation and educational NLP applications. I also work in the field of argumentation and I'm trying to understand how we can improve the language reasoning of the models using argomentative data. Finally I focus on the evalutation of open ended generation, particularly assessing the reliability of LLMs as Judges.
